About Us

2010 was the year when Cotton Rose started its operation as a sourcing agent of Ready-Made Garments. It is almost ten years we are serving several clients in different capacities with the vertical area with a good reputation.
The modest beginnings, notwithstanding, Cotton Rose has grown in over the years.
